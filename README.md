# 🎬 Social Movie Club

Hello!

This project is my personal playground where I can experiment and make continuous improvements. Inside the code, you will see many comments, notes like "Why did I do this here?" or questions to myself like "Is there a better way to do this?".

My main goal is to improve these parts over time and turn every step into a technical document by analyzing what I changed.

### 🧪 Learning Journey and Experiments
This is not a "production-ready" application. Instead, it is a learning journey that I care about and I know it will never truly "end". Here is what I focus on:

* **Freedom to Make Mistakes & Refactoring:** I always ask myself, "If I can't do it, why not?" and try to find the answer.
* **Learning by "Breaking":** Sometimes I choose the most complex way on purpose. Then, while simplifying it, I take notes about that "Aha!" moment inside the code.
* **Technology Trials:** I am testing new techniques while building bridges between Spring Boot and Next.js.

### 🛠️ Current Status
Right now, I am working on movie lists, fetching dynamic movies from an API, user following systems, and synchronizing the like system between frontend and backend. Some things are not "perfect" yet, but they get better with every commit.

### 📝 My Notes
I leave comments about the things I learned and the "So, that’s why!" moments throughout the project. If you see a strange solution somewhere, I am probably trying to understand something or pushing my limits.

### 🧪 Öğrenme Yolculuğu ve Deneyler
Bu bir "production-ready" uygulamadan ziyade, bitmeyeceğini bildiğim ve çok önemsediğim bir öğrenme yolculuğu. Burada şunlara odaklanıyorum:

* **Hata Yapma Özgürlüğü & Refactoring:** "Yapamıyorsam neden yapamıyorum?" sorusunun peşinden gidiyorum.
* **"Kırarak" Öğrenme:** Bazen bir özelliği bilerek en karmaşık yoldan yapıyorum, sonra onu en sade haline getirirken aradaki o 'aydınlanma' anını kodun içine not alıyorum.
* **Teknoloji Denemeleri:** Spring Boot ve Next.js arasındaki köprüleri kurarken yeni teknikler deniyorum.
